Business Scope

Trademark Agency

Specifically refers to the rights of trademark holders or its successor in interest, within the legal time limit for registration of a trademark.

  • Trademark registration consulting, trademark query, logo design.
  • Acting trademark registration, change registration, renewal of registration, transfer registration.
  • Trademark Application objection and the respondent, the respondent and the revocation of the trademark review, trademark application for review and the respondent, and the respondent trademark application for invalidation.
  • Agent enterprises trademark negotiations, drafting and signing of the transfer of trademarks, trademark licensing contracts.
  • Brand protection for enterprises to develop strategic plans to apply for China famous brands.
  • As a corporate trademark counsel to provide legal advice and service.
